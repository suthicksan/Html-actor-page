Joseph Vijay Chandrasekhar, known professionally as Vijay, is a prominent Indian actor and playback singer who works in Tamil cinema. Born on June 22, 1974, in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, Vijay has become one of the most commercially successful actors in Tamil cinema, often referred to by his fans as "Thalapathy" (commander). Career Highlights: Early Career: Vijay made his debut as a child actor in the Tamil film "Vetri" (1984), directed by his father, S. A. Chandrasekhar. He transitioned to lead roles with the film "Naalaiya Theerpu" (1992) at the age of 18. Breakthrough: His breakthrough came with the romantic film "Poove Unakkaga" (1996), which established him as a leading actor in Tamil cinema. Commercial Success: Vijay has starred in numerous successful films, including "Ghilli" (2004), "Thuppakki" (2012), "Mersal" (2017), and "Master" (2021). His films often feature a mix of action, romance, and social messages. Awards and Recognition: Vijay has received several awards, including the Tamil Nadu State Film Awards and Vijay Awards. He was also honored with the Kalaimamani award by the Government of Tamil Nadu in 1998. Personal Life: Vijay is married to Sangeeta Sornalingam, and they have two children. He is known for his philanthropic activities and has a significant fan following, especially among the youth. Political Involvement: In February 2024, Vijay announced his retirement from films and entered politics by launching the Tamilaga Vettri Kazhagam (TVK) party. Vijay's journey from a child actor to a superstar and now a politician is truly inspiring. His contributions to Tamil cinema and his influence on his fans are remarkable.
